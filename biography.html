Tobi Olamide Alabi

Popularly known as TCC, is the founder of the award winning pageant/model grooming and lifestyle brand TCCBRAND formerly known as THECATWALKCOACH. He started his career back in 2014 as a model in Accra, Ghana and walked on reputable fashion shows in and outside Nigeria.

TCC is a brand that was launched to help groom and educate aspiring models and beauty Queens on the importance of catwalk, etiquette, poise, posture, styling and charisma etc. as he believes proper catwalk gives a model more appraisal on the runway than height and skin color.

This brand also focuses on non pageant models/beauty queens as an everyday woman needs to find her rhythm as well, in order to have a top notch confidence level.

In 2020, he became the first "African Catwalk Instructor" to be listed on he New-York walk magazine as the worlds "Top 100 Creatives" and was the only catwalk instructor to be featured.

He is the official groomer for MISS WORLD NIGERIA 2021 & 2022, MISS WORLD LIBERIA 2022, MISS GLOBE JAPAN 2022, MISS GLOBE GHANA 2022, MISS GRAND NIGERIA 2020 & 2021, THE NIGERIAN QUEEN Pageant camp 2020 & 2021, FACE OF IDOMA CARNIVAL INTERNATIONAL Pageant 2021 & 2022 MISS SUPRANATIONAL NIGERIA 2021 & 2022, FACE OF ZIKEL 2020 & 2022 and many more, with either a win, an award or a placement on the international stage.

In 2020, he became the first instructor to groom a delegate who became the first Nigerian to place in the "TOP 20" at the international pageant. He has worked with so many model agencies in Nigeria and beyond, to list a few: gngmodelsafrica and Newfame models.

Other achievements are: Eko Heritage Award for Pageant Icon of the Year, The Collection Award for Pageant Coah of the Year.
