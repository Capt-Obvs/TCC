Question: How can I book for training?

Bookings are made by selecting a particular training package in the "Programs" section.

Question: When is the next training starting?

Trainings are all year round, there is no specific start date. It starts when the registration slots opened are filled up. You can only have a specific date if you are enrolling for a private training.

Question: How do I make payments?

Payment can be made on the website after you are done selecting the training you wish to enroll for.

Question: Are there any available discounts?

No, there are no discounts. Training fees are fixed and are same for every trainee in the different categories.

Question: Is there a number I can call?

Consultation requests are available at a cost. For consultation bookings, kindly send a message to @tccorganization on Instagram.
