Standard Training Session

Duration - One Month
Timeframe - Three times weekly (12 sessions: weekends only)
Daily hours - 2hrs
Fee - $261
Minimum no of trainees - 10
Maximum no of trainees - 20

Important Notes:

• Standard trainings are fixed classes that hold at a particular time every month and cannot be altered.

• Payment for the standard class is universal and non-negotiable, every student attending this class pays the same fee and are taught the same thing.

• The standard class teaches you a sweet yet classy and energetic catwalk, for pageant models, brides, fashion models, everyday women e.t.c.

• You do not need to have any experience in catwalk to enroll in the standard class, it is open and very accomodating to trainees who never walked on heels.

• After the standard class, you must have learnt the basics to having a sweet catwalk for your personal use.

Scheduled Training Session

Duration - Two/Three Weeks
Timeframe - 15 sessions: weekends only
Daily hours - 3hrs
Fee - $556
Minimum no of trainees - 5
Maximum no of trainees - 12

Important Notes:

• The scheduled trainings hold back to back non-stop till the syllabus is completed, which means it can work for late minute pageant models going for a pageantry in a short time.

• The scheduled trainings teaches you sultry catwalk that has to do with waist movement, hip to knee body balance and facial expressions.

• The scheduled trainings package is 80% more beneficial to pageant and fashion models than everyday women.

• Classes are fixed based on the availability of the coach at the time of payment, trainees are not allowed to choose a time for themselves.

• Payment for the scheduled trainings is universal, every trainee has to pay a compulsory fee. However, payments can be done in 2 installments and must be fully paid before the class commences.

Private Training Session

Duration - One Month
Timeframe - 15 sessions: weekdays & weekends only
Daily hours - 2hrs
Fee - $1278
Minimum no of trainees - 1
Maximum no of trainees - 3

Important Notes:

• Private trainings are singular, this means it gives you the option of training alone and learningis faster this way.

• Private trainings can be scheduled based on the availability of the trainee and not on the availability of the coach alone and any classes missed by the trainee cannot be replaced.

• Private trainings for catwalk is an intense training that allows you to learn the proper catwalk types and helps you learn faster as you are focused. Other benefits of this trainings are: sultry catwalk, swimsuit catwalk, daily catwalk.

• Payment for private trainings is split into two payment installments, first payment as registration and second payment before the class starts. Any default in payment atrracts a penalty.

Advanced Training Session

Duration - Two Weeks
Timeframe - 10 sessions: virtual & physical
Daily hours - 3hrs physical : 1hr virtual
Fee - $697

Important Notes:

• Advanced trainings are only available to trainees going going on the international stage to represent a country, trainees who have trained with TCC prior to new registration and finally trainees who have been pageant title holders in the past and are returning for a national pageant.

• The advanced training session is a combination of both virtual and physical classes. Here is how it works: From the total number of classes, 70% will be phyhsical while the reminaing 30% is virtual.

• Payment for this training is split into two installments and must be fully paid once half of the session is done.
